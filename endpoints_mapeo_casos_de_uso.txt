Mapeo de Endpoints y Casos de Uso Implementados
===============================================

---

Autenticación y usuarios
------------------------
CU01 Iniciar sesión:
  POST /api/login/
CU02 Cerrar sesión:
  POST /api/logout/
CU03 Recuperar Contraseña:
  POST /api/password-reset/
  POST /api/password-reset/confirm/
CU04 Registrar Cuenta de Cliente:
  POST /api/register/
CU06 Gestionar Clientes (CRUD):
  GET, POST /api/clients/
  GET, PUT, PATCH, DELETE /api/clients/{id}/

---

Productos, Marcas y Garantías
-----------------------------
CU05 Gestionar Productos (CRUD):
  GET, POST /api/products/products/
  GET, PUT, PATCH, DELETE /api/products/products/{id}/
CU13 Buscar Productos (Texto):
  GET /api/products/products/?search=...
CU23 Gestionar Categorías (CRUD):
  GET, POST /api/products/categories/
  GET, PUT, PATCH, DELETE /api/products/categories/{slug}/
CU24 Gestionar Marcas (CRUD):
  GET, POST /api/products/brands/
  GET, PUT, PATCH, DELETE /api/products/brands/{id}/
CU25 Gestionar Garantías (CRUD):
  GET, POST /api/products/warranties/
  GET, PUT, PATCH, DELETE /api/products/warranties/{id}/

---

Ventas, Carrito y Pagos
-----------------------
CU07 Consultar Historial de Ventas:
  GET /api/sales/sales-history/
CU15/CU16 Gestionar Carrito de Compra:
  GET, POST /api/sales/cart/
  PUT, DELETE /api/sales/cart/items/{item_id}/
CU18 Realizar Pago de Compra:
  POST /api/sales/checkout/
CU19 Recibir Comprobante de Venta:
  GET /api/sales/sales-history/{order_id}/receipt/
CU26 Gestionar Métodos de Pago (CRUD):
  GET, POST /api/sales/config/payment-methods/
  GET, PUT, PATCH, DELETE /api/sales/config/payment-methods/{id}/
